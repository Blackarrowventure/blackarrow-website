📊 Detailed Comparison

⚪ ORIGINAL (All Gold)

  • Buttons blend with headings
  • All accents same color
  • Less visual hierarchy
  • Single-color aesthetic
  • Can feel monotonous
  • Good for minimalist
  • Less clickable appearance

🟢 EMERALD GREEN

  • Modern & tech-forward
  • Highest contrast available
  • Best CTR (Click-Through Rate)
  • Perfect gold + green pairing
  • Premium yet professional
  • Great for tech companies
  • Color-blind accessible

🔵 SAPPHIRE BLUE

  • Corporate & trustworthy
  • Very professional
  • Apple/Microsoft style
  • Good contrast & readability
  • Premium feel
  • More conservative
  • Enterprise-grade look

Which Color Do You Prefer?

Both are professional and modern. Choose based on your brand personality:

My Recommendation: Emerald Green for a tech/infrastructure company
But Sapphire Blue works great too if you want more corporate feel
