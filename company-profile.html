Who We Are

About Us

Black Arrow Venture is a Saudi-based trading and solutions company dedicated to delivering comprehensive infrastructure and technology solutions across the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. With a strategic focus on Electric Vehicle (EV) infrastructure, Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) systems, HVAC solutions, and specialised engineering products, we provide complete, end-to-end services tailored to modern market demands.

Operating in alignment with Saudi Vision 2030, Black Arrow Venture actively supports the Kingdom's transformation toward sustainable energy, smart infrastructure, and diversified economic growth. Our approach integrates supply, installation, testing & commissioning, and long-term maintenance — ensuring reliability, efficiency, and operational excellence for every project.

We serve a wide range of sectors, including commercial, industrial, hospitality, healthcare, and public infrastructure, delivering solutions that meet international standards while addressing local regulatory and operational requirements.

Driven by innovation, quality, and strong partnerships, Black Arrow Venture is committed to building resilient systems that empower businesses, enable clean mobility, and support the future-ready infrastructure of Saudi Arabia.
